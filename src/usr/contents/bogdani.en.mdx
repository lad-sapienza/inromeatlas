# Julian Bogdani

My research interests include Classical Archaeology, Archaeology of Ancient Epirus, Fortifications and building techniques, topography and methodologies for archaeological survey, IT applications to the archaeological research such as databases, GIS, Web technologies, etc.

I teach “Methodogies for Archaeological Research”, “Digital Archaeology”, “Digital Humanities”, and “GIS for Archaeology” at Sapienza University of Rome.

ORCiD: 0000-0001-5250-927X

Email: julian.bogdani@uniroma1.it

Current position

    Associate Professor of Methodogies for Archaeological Research at Sapienza University of Rome
    Director of the laboratory: LAD Laboratory for Digital Archaeology at Sapienza (https://lad.saras.uniroma1.it/).
    Director or the Archaeological Project of Sapienza Univesity of Rome in  Çuka e Ajtoit (Albania, https://lad.saras.uniroma1.it/ricerca/missione-archeologica-sapienza-a-cuka-e-ajtoit-albania/)
    Co-editor of Groma Documenting Archaeology, an open access peer-reviewed e-journal  focusing on the different methodologies applied to archaeology. (https://archaeopresspublishing.com/ojs/index.php/groma/)
    P.I. of the research project funded by Sapienza University of Rome ‘Kestríne: un paesaggio di frontiera attraverso gli imperi” (Middle-size project 2021)
    P.I. of the research project funded by Sapienza University of Rome ‘Ricostruzione dei paesaggi storici e archeologici dell'Albania meridionale attraverso l'utilizzo di fotografie aeree storiche della Seconda Guerra Mondiale’ (Small-size project 2022).
